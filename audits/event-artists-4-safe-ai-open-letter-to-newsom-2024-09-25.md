Source content: Newsom's veto message: "By focusing only on the most expensive and large-scale models, SB 1047 establishes a regulatory framework that could give the public a false sense of security about controlling this fast-moving technology" and "SB 1047 does not take into account whether an AI system is deployed in high-risk environments, involves critical decision-making or the use of sensitive data." The two surfaces (veto message and veto-day announcement) are conjoined in the body without explicit separation. The body's "empirical trajectory analysis" is closer to the announcement's language than the veto message's; however, the announcement and the veto were released together as a coordinated communication, and the substantive framing claim is faithful to that package.
