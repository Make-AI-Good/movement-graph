## Claim 10: "A revised 90 MW scheme was then called in and approved by Secretary of State Angela Rayner in 2024"

Source: https://bebeez.eu/2025/07/12/angela-rayner-greenlights-twice-rejected-buckinghamshire-data-center/
Source content: Article dated 12 July 2025 reports: "A renewed application, lodged in 2024 and also refused by the council, was 'called in' by the Ministry of Housing, Communities and Local Government, giving Mrs Rayner the final say. A five-day public inquiry was held in December 2024, following which the planning inspector appointed by the Secretary of State recommended allowing the appeal and granting permission for the development." Article's framing places the approval in 2025, with the inquiry in December 2024.
Comparison: The body conflates two distinct events — the application was lodged and call-in occurred in 2024, but the approval itself was in 2025 (after a December 2024 public inquiry). "Called in and approved … in 2024" is therefore factually wrong as to the approval year. The Leigh Day press release (a separate cited source) corroborates the 2025 approval by referencing it as the subject of a January 2026 judicial review.
Decision: discrepancy
