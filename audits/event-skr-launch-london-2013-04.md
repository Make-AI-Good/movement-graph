## Claim 1: "On Monday 22 and Tuesday 23 April 2013, the Campaign to Stop Killer Robots held its global public launch in London"

Source: https://www.stopkillerrobots.org/news/press-release-urgent-action-needed-to-ban-fully-autonomous-weapons-non-governmental-organizations-convene-to-launch-campaign-to-stop-killer-robots/ and https://www.hrw.org/news/2013/04/23/arms-new-campaign-stop-killer-robots
Source content: Press release dated 23 April 2013 names London as the launch venue and confirms the Steering Committee "convened a conference on April 22"; HRW: "Civil society will lead the way to press governments to ban fully autonomous weapons, Human Rights Watch said today at the launch of the global Campaign to Stop Killer Robots."
Comparison: Both sources confirm a two-day London launch on 22-23 April 2013.
Decision: verified
