## Claim 12: "only a 0.31% reduction in Scope 1 emissions since the 2019 baseline"

Source: https://www.computerweekly.com/news/366627215/Google-slams-claims-its-misleading-the-public-with-its-GHG-emissions-data

Comparison: The body asserts 0.31% is the *magnitude* of the Scope 1 reduction since 2019. The source instead says 0.31% is the *share* of Google's total 2025 emissions that Scope 1 represents — a separate quantity. The source confirms Scope 1 has decreased since 2019 but does not quantify the reduction at 0.31%; the 0.31% figure in the source is the share of total emissions, not the percentage reduction. The body therefore misattributes what the 0.31% quantifies.
Decision: correction
