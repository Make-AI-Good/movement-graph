## Claim 19: scalar:location — "Utrecht, Netherlands (Stichting PauseAI [registered in Utrecht]; the Dutch chapter operates nationally, with public actions concentrated in The Hague and Amsterdam)"

Source: https://pauseai.info/legal
Source content: Stichting PauseAI's registered address is "Box C5957, Kwikstaartlaan 42, 3704GS Zeist, The Netherlands" — Zeist, not Utrecht. The body of this same entity explicitly acknowledges: "The KVK-registered mailing address is a Zeist PO box, ~10 km east of Utrecht; the foundation's day-to-day operating address... sits in Utrecht at Skopjestraat 26, 3541ES."
Comparison: The location scalar's "Stichting PauseAI registered in Utrecht" plain-reads as the city of Utrecht; the canonical source and the entity's own body both place the formal KVK registration at a Zeist address. The Utrecht link is the operating address, not the registered one. (Zeist is in Utrecht Province but is a separate municipality.) Recommended replacement: "Stichting PauseAI operates from Utrecht" (or "registered in Zeist; operating from Utrecht"), preserving the parenthetical's intent without contradicting the body.
Decision: discrepancy
