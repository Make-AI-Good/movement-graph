Source: Secondary canonical reporting (consulted pass 1: The Conversation, Times of Israel) referencing Peña Nieto's response timeline.
Source content: Reporting indicates Peña Nieto's office issued a same-day denial. The acknowledgment that the government had purchased Pegasus and the formal Attorney General referral came at a 22 June press conference, three days after the launch — "in his June press conference on the subject, Peña Nieto promised an investigation... then warned that the attorney general's office would 'apply the law against those who have levelled false accusations against the government'."
Comparison: The same-day denial is corroborated. The body's compressed framing of the Attorney General announcement as part of the same-day response is not cleanly supported by canonical sources, which consistently locate the AG referral at the 22 June press conference. Judgment-loaded edge — without a 19 June primary source confirming the same-day Attorney General announcement, the precise timing is unverifiable.
Decision: uncorroborated
