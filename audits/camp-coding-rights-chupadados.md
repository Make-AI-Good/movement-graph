## Claim 1: "Chupadados — Portuguese for 'data-suckers'"

Source: https://chupadados.codingrights.org/
Source content: The chupadados homepage uses the term "chupadados" and presents the project's framing as "A face oculta das nossas tecnologias de estimação" (the hidden face of our beloved technologies); the "chupadados (data-sucker)" gloss personifies pervasive data extraction. Corroborated on the LatAm Journalism Review feature which uses "Datasucker"/"datasuckers" throughout (e.g. "Mother in a click: pregnancy as a jackpot for the Datasucker").

Decision: corroborated
