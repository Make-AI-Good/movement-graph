## Claim 1: "27th president of SAG-AFTRA" (body paragraph 1, "Organisational vehicle" section)

Source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fran_Drescher and https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/SAG-AFTRA
Source content: Wikipedia Fran_Drescher: "3rd National President of SAG–AFTRA: In office October 15, 2021 – September 12, 2025." Wikipedia SAG-AFTRA confirms only three presidents since the 2012 merger preceded her: Ken Howard (2012–2016), Gabrielle Carteris (2016–2021), Drescher (2021–2025), with Sean Astin succeeding her. Wikipedia notes there were 28 SAG presidents and 22 AFTRA presidents *before* the 2012 merger, but those were the predecessor unions; the merged SAG-AFTRA's president count starts at Ken Howard.
Comparison: Body twice claims "27th president of SAG-AFTRA" (paragraph 1 and "Organisational vehicle" section). Wikipedia (both Fran_Drescher and SAG-AFTRA articles) records her as the 3rd president of the merged SAG-AFTRA. The "27th" figure appears to conflate SAG (predecessor union) numbering with SAG-AFTRA numbering. **Mirror in frontmatter scalar `sources[].note` for the Wikipedia Fran_Drescher source (line 41): "the 15 October 2021 inauguration as SAG-AFTRA's 27th president" — same single-token correction (`27th` → `3rd`) applies to the scalar. Single-token replacement: change all three "27th" → "3rd".
Decision: correction
