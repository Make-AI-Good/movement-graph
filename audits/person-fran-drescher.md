Source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fran_Drescher; corroborated by https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/SAG-AFTRA
Source content: Drescher Wikipedia entry states she is the "3rd National President" of SAG-AFTRA. The SAG-AFTRA Wikipedia article lists only four post-merger presidents in order: "1. Ken Howard (2012–2016) 2. Gabrielle Carteris (2016–2021) 3. Fran Drescher (2021–2025) 4. Sean Astin (2025–present)" — placing Drescher third.
Comparison: SAG-AFTRA is the post-2012 merger entity and has had only four National Presidents to date; the body's "27th" is implausible for a union that has existed since 2012. The "27" likely originates from a cumulative-SAG numbering (SAG had ~25 presidents 1933–2012), but the body's phrasing — "27th president of SAG-AFTRA" — attaches the number to the merged union, where it is not the count.
Decision: correction
