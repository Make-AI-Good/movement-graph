Source: https://algorithmwatch.org/en/eu-artificial-intelligence-act-for-fundamental-rights/
Source content: The seven prohibitions in the listing order are: (1) "social scoring systems"; (2) "remote biometric identification in publicly accessible spaces (by all actors)"; (3) "emotion recognition systems"; (4) "discriminatory biometric categorisation"; (5) "AI physiognomy"; (6) "systems used to predict future criminal activity"; (7) "systems to profile and risk-assess in a migration context".
