# Damar Juniarto

Damar Juniarto is the principal Indonesian civil-society voice on internet access, freedom of expression, online safety, and — since 2024 — the use of AI and digital technology in Indonesian democracy. He is tracked here as a Voice because his sustained on-record output — own-platform essays on [Medium](https://damarjuniarto.medium.com/ai-and-the-2024-indonesian-election-are-ethical-guidelines-enough-e777d8d50851) and a long-running [WordPress site](https://damarjuniarto.wordpress.com/2026/02/11/pikat-demokrasi-dari-indonesia-untuk-dunia/) (both English- and Indonesian-language), an English-language [SAFEnet author archive](https://safenet.or.id/author/damar/) running across the SAFEnet decade, on-record statements in [Reuters](https://x.com/oomdamar/status/1948193451486642575), AP, [Rest of World](https://restofworld.org/profile/damar-juniarto/), [ARTICLE 19](https://www.article19.org/resources/activist-interview-damar-juniarto-of-safenet-indonesia/), and [Arena](https://arena.org.au/dead-deepfake-dictators-and-declining-digi-optimism-in-indonesia/), a [panellist seat](https://damarjuniarto.wordpress.com/2026/02/11/pikat-demokrasi-dari-indonesia-untuk-dunia/) at the late-January 2024 Indonesian Fifth Presidential Debate, an [external-consultant appointment](../persons/person-damar-juniarto.md) to the UN Secretary-General's AI Advisory Body since 2024, and a [coordinator role at KTP2JB](https://ktp2jb.or.id/id/anggota/damar-juniarto) — has done more than any other Indonesian organiser's to install into Indonesian, Southeast Asian, and Global-South discourse the working framings under which the corpus's digital-rights and AI-and-democracy registers operate from the Indonesian vantage (see [Person entry](../persons/person-damar-juniarto.md)).

He is the corpus's first Indonesia-anchored Voice and the first Southeast Asia voice outside Philippines-anchored [voice-chat-garcia-ramilo](../voices/voice-chat-garcia-ramilo.md). He carries two distinct registers across one career arc: the freedom-of-expression / ITE-Law / [digital-authoritarianism](https://medium.com/@DamarJuniarto/at-the-brink-of-digital-authoritarian-c43d57e862) register he installed across his 2013-2023 leadership of [SAFEnet (Southeast Asia Freedom of Expression Network)](../organizations/org-safenet.md), and the [AI-and-democracy / press-as-fourth-pillar](https://damarjuniarto.wordpress.com/2026/02/09/memudarnya-peran-pers-sebagai-pilar-demokrasi-akibat-dominasi-ai/) register he has articulated since founding [PIKAT Demokrasi](https://damarjuniarto.wordpress.com/2026/02/11/pikat-demokrasi-dari-indonesia-untuk-dunia/) in January 2024.

## Signature framings

Four framings in Juniarto's public output have travelled beyond his own platforms into Indonesian, Southeast Asian, and Global-South discourse.

- **"Digital authoritarianism."** This is his single most-cited pre-PIKAT framing and the through-line of his SAFEnet-era output. He uses it to name a pattern — that Indonesian digital-rights erosion is not incidental but [structurally directed](https://www.indoleft.org/news/2022-07-11/internet-watchdog-warns-indonesia-heading-towards-digital-authoritarianism.html) toward authoritarian re-tooling of the digital sphere. In his Medium essay [*At the Brink of Digital Authoritarianism*](https://medium.com/@DamarJuniarto/at-the-brink-of-digital-authoritarian-c43d57e862) he gave the framing its earliest sustained published statement, and the SAFEnet [*Rise of Digital Authoritarianism in Indonesia* dispatch](https://safenet.or.id/author/damar/) (December 2020) carries the same line into the SAFEnet annual-report register. Indoleft's July 2022 coverage captures his working formulation: ["what has occurred is in fact an erosion of citizens' rights which is heading in the direction of what we have been afraid of, namely digital authoritarianism"](https://www.indoleft.org/news/2022-07-11/internet-watchdog-warns-indonesia-heading-towards-digital-authoritarianism.html).
- **"Three-axis digital repression."** Across SAFEnet's annual *Digital Rights Situation Report of Indonesia* series, Juniarto codified [three intersecting axes](https://safenet.or.id/2022/03/in-indonesia-digital-repression-is-keep-continues/) — access to the internet, freedom of expression, and online safety — under which Indonesian digital-rights tracking organises. The framing is the conceptual backbone of the SAFEnet annual report and has anchored the network's Southeast Asia regional-monitoring posture; he carried the same schema into his ARTICLE 19 interview, [rating Indonesia "On a scale of 0 to 10 ... at 4"](https://www.article19.org/resources/activist-interview-damar-juniarto-of-safenet-indonesia/) on freedom-of-expression and explicitly tying the rating to Article 27(3) of the ITE Law.
- **"The press as the fourth pillar of democracy in the age of AI."** This is the central framing of his post-PIKAT register. In his February 2026 essay [*Memudarnya Peran Pers Sebagai Pilar Demokrasi Akibat Dominasi AI*](https://damarjuniarto.wordpress.com/2026/02/09/memudarnya-peran-pers-sebagai-pilar-demokrasi-akibat-dominasi-ai/) — the Indonesian-language statement of the line — and in the [companion essay](https://damarjuniarto.wordpress.com/2026/02/23/india-ai-impact-summit-peran-pers-yang-menurun-dan-tata-kelola-ai-masa-depan/) on the India AI Impact Summit, he names the decline of journalism as a democratic institution under generative-AI-shaped media environments as the through-line PIKAT's 2024-2025 research line tracks. The framing connects his earlier digital-rights register (where press freedom was one axis among several) to the AI-and-democracy register PIKAT now anchors.
- **"Indonesia is not ready to be an AI developer."** This is his signature framing on the Indonesian national AI-strategy debate of 2025-2026. In a [Reuters quote he himself amplified on X](https://x.com/oomdamar/status/1948193451486642575) he frames Indonesia's posture against its own developer-nation ambitions — pointing at chip-infrastructure absence and AI-skills gaps in the workforce — and ties the assessment to PIKAT's AI-safety-monitoring brief. The framing is the corpus's clearest Indonesian-civil-society voice on the national-AI-strategy debate.

## Public output and venues

Juniarto's public-facing work spans five overlapping channels.

- **Own WordPress site (long-running, English and Indonesian).** His [personal WordPress](https://damarjuniarto.wordpress.com/) is the longest-running of his own platforms, running back through his SAFEnet years and forward into the PIKAT period. The site carries his biographical record, his framing essays (including the [*PIKAT Demokrasi — Dari Indonesia Untuk Dunia*](https://damarjuniarto.wordpress.com/2026/02/11/pikat-demokrasi-dari-indonesia-untuk-dunia/) February 2026 founding-rationale piece), and his Indonesian-language essays on the [*Memudarnya Peran Pers*](https://damarjuniarto.wordpress.com/2026/02/09/memudarnya-peran-pers-sebagai-pilar-demokrasi-akibat-dominasi-ai/) press-as-fourth-pillar line and on the [*India AI Impact Summit*](https://damarjuniarto.wordpress.com/2026/02/23/india-ai-impact-summit-peran-pers-yang-menurun-dan-tata-kelola-ai-masa-depan/) multilateral-AI-governance commentary. The Indonesian-language register is rare in the corpus.
- **Medium (English-language op-eds).** His [Medium account](https://damarjuniarto.medium.com/) carries the English-language op-ed register of his output. The most-cited Medium pieces are [*AI and the 2024 Indonesian Election: Are Ethical Guidelines Enough?*](https://damarjuniarto.medium.com/ai-and-the-2024-indonesian-election-are-ethical-guidelines-enough-e777d8d50851) (20 February 2024), which carries his post-Presidential-Debate framing that voluntary AI-ethics codes are insufficient; [*The Use of AI in Indonesia Election 2024: A Case Study*](https://damarjuniarto.medium.com/the-use-of-ai-in-indonesia-election-2024-a-case-study-07feec783a10) (24 May 2024), which enumerates the Jokowi-Mandarin, Suharto-reanimation, and Prabowo-Arabic deepfake cases; and the earlier [*At the Brink of Digital Authoritarianism*](https://medium.com/@DamarJuniarto/at-the-brink-of-digital-authoritarian-c43d57e862) which gave his SAFEnet-era signature framing its sustained published statement.
- **SAFEnet author archive (2017-2022, English).** His [English-language author archive](https://safenet.or.id/author/damar/) on SAFEnet's site is the systematic record of his SAFEnet-era output. The archive holds [*Indonesia Government Has To Stop Blocking Telegram Messenger*](https://safenet.or.id/author/damar/) (July 2017), [*Perspective: Challenges in Strengthening Digital Security in Southeast Asia countries*](https://safenet.or.id/author/damar/) (November 2019), [*Internet Shutdown in Indonesia: The New Normal Policy to Censor Information Online?*](https://safenet.or.id/author/damar/) (October 2020), [*The Rise of Digital Authoritarianism in Indonesia*](https://safenet.or.id/author/damar/) (December 2020), and [*Online identity-based violence prevention in Southeast Asia*](https://safenet.or.id/author/damar/) (2022) — the chronological trace of the SAFEnet decade as he framed it from inside the organisation.
- **External press as named source.** Across the SAFEnet decade and into the PIKAT period, Juniarto has been the regular Indonesian civil-society voice quoted in mainstream and specialist press: [Rest of World's 2022 RoW100 Global Tech Changemakers profile](https://restofworld.org/profile/damar-juniarto/) framed him as "an optimist" against Indonesia's middling Freedom House rating; the [ARTICLE 19 activist interview](https://www.article19.org/resources/activist-interview-damar-juniarto-of-safenet-indonesia/) gave the long-form treatment of the SAFEnet trajectory; Zacharias Szumer's December 2024 [Arena long-form](https://arena.org.au/dead-deepfake-dictators-and-declining-digi-optimism-in-indonesia/) carries the "I finally saw the dark side of internet freedom" line that marks his shift from the earlier optimist register; [Reuters' 2025 coverage](https://x.com/oomdamar/status/1948193451486642575) of the Indonesian national-AI-strategy debate quotes his PIKAT-era "not ready to be an AI developer" framing; and [Indoleft's 2022 coverage](https://www.indoleft.org/news/2022-07-11/internet-watchdog-warns-indonesia-heading-towards-digital-authoritarianism.html) recorded the "heading toward digital authoritarianism" line.
- **Indonesian Presidential Debate, UN, KTP2JB.** Juniarto's institutional platforms add the multilateral and Indonesian-government-mediated venues to the own-platform and press registers above. His January 2024 [panellist seat](https://damarjuniarto.wordpress.com/2026/02/11/pikat-demokrasi-dari-indonesia-untuk-dunia/) at the KPU Fifth Presidential Debate is the moment he points to as PIKAT's origin point. His 2024 appointment as external consultant to the [UN Secretary-General's AI Advisory Body](../persons/person-damar-juniarto.md) and his September 2024 coordinator role on the digital-platform-cooperation division of [KTP2JB](https://ktp2jb.or.id/id/anggota/damar-juniarto), Indonesia's quality-journalism digital-platform-governance committee, give the post-PIKAT register its formal institutional carrier.

## Organisational vehicles

Juniarto's public output runs through three named organisational vehicles. [SAFEnet](../organizations/org-safenet.md), the network he [founded in 2013](https://safenet.or.id/about-us/) and led through the end of 2023, is the principal organisational anchor of his SAFEnet-decade output: the annual *Digital Rights Situation Report of Indonesia* series, the four-pillar policy-advocacy / victim-support / capacity-building / solidarity programme, and the holistic-security training register he established post-2019. [PIKAT Demokrasi](https://damarjuniarto.wordpress.com/2026/02/11/pikat-demokrasi-dari-indonesia-untuk-dunia/) — Pusat Inovasi Kecerdasan Artifisial dan Teknologi untuk Demokrasi — is the AI-and-democracy research-institute vehicle he founded in January 2024 and inside which his post-2024 register is structured. The [UN Secretary-General's AI Advisory Body](../persons/person-damar-juniarto.md) external-consultant seat and the [KTP2JB](https://ktp2jb.or.id/id/anggota/damar-juniarto) coordinator role give him institutional positions on the multilateral and Indonesian-platform-governance sides of the AI-governance debate.

## Why this is a Voice entry

A Voice entry is created here, rather than additional structure on the [Person entry](../persons/person-damar-juniarto.md), because Juniarto's public output is itself the load-bearing object the corpus needs to track: the working civil-society framing of Indonesian digital-rights advocacy and Indonesian AI-and-democracy posture — "digital authoritarianism", the three-axis digital-repression schema, "the press as the fourth pillar of democracy in the age of AI", and "Indonesia is not ready to be an AI developer" — is the language he installed into Indonesian, Southeast Asian, and Global-South discourse across more than a decade as SAFEnet's founding Executive Director and, since January 2024, as PIKAT Demokrasi's founder. The corpus's [SAFEnet](../organizations/org-safenet.md) cluster carries no other Voice anchor, and the corpus carries no other Indonesian-language Voice; this entry gives both the SAFEnet thread and the Indonesian-vantage AI-and-democracy register a first-person voice. Affiliation and biographical structure are recorded on the linked [Person entry](../persons/person-damar-juniarto.md) per the corpus's Person/Voice split.
