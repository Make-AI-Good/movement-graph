# Lightspeed Grants

Lightspeed Grants was a rapid-grant programme launched in June 2023 by [Lightcone Infrastructure](https://www.lesswrong.com/) — the organisation that operates LessWrong and the Alignment Forum — with [Jaan Tallinn](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jaan_Tallinn) (co-founder of Skype and Kazaa, also the primary donor behind the [Survival and Flourishing Fund](fund-survival-and-flourishing-fund.md)) as the primary funder. The programme offered $5 million in its inaugural round and aimed to move faster than existing grant vehicles, with [decisions within 60 days](https://lightspeedgrants.org/) (14 days for "venture grants") — to reach projects that the slower cycles of the [Long-Term Future Fund](fund-long-term-future-fund.md) or the [Survival and Flourishing Fund](fund-survival-and-flourishing-fund.md) might miss.

## Focus and mechanics

The programme prioritised four areas: reducing existential risk from advanced AI; preventing the development or spread of novel biological pathogens; improving reasoning among global decision-makers; and uncovering crucial considerations about humanity's long-term future. Applications outside these areas were also welcomed. Fiscal sponsorship for recipients lacking charitable status was provided by Hack Club Bank.

Approximately five evaluators — selected for reasoning ability and networks relevant to the programme's focus areas, in collaboration with the funders — assessed applications using a graphical tool specifying funding preferences across projects. A coordinated allocation algorithm distributed funds in a way designed to prevent double-funding while maximising funder preferences.

## Status and corpus connection

Lightspeed Grants ran one documented round, with applications closing July 6 2023 and decisions communicated by August 6 2023. The programme had not relaunched as of 2026. Among this corpus's in-scope organisations, [PauseAI](../organizations/org-pauseai.md) is a documented recipient, [receiving €18,410](https://pauseai.info/funding) from Lightspeed Grants.
