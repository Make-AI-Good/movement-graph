# Anna Krolik

Anna Krolik serves as Director of [MIT AI Alignment (MAIA)](../local-groups/lg-mit-ai-alignment.md), leading the student group's executive board. MAIA runs AI safety fellowship programmes, a research mentorship track, and semi-annual retreats connecting MIT students with the wider AI-safety community.
