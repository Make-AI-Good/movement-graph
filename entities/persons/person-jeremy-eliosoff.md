# Jeremy Eliosoff

Jeremy Eliosoff is the national leader of [PauseAI Canada](../local-groups/lg-pauseai-canada.md), the Canadian national chapter of [PauseAI](../organizations/org-pauseai.md). [Named by PauseAI Global as Canada's national leader](https://pauseai.info/about) in the federation's chapter directory, he is based in Montréal, Québec. A computer animation professional and visual effects artist — with [screen credits](https://www.imdb.com/name/nm1990871/) spanning film and television and a background including Fine Arts from Concordia University and Computer Animation from Sheridan College — he also maintains a fine-arts practice in dry pastels. He [spoke with Robert Wright on the NonZero podcast in May 2026](https://www.nonzero.org/p/a-chat-with-the-head-of-pauseai-canada) on AI safety, the PauseAI movement, and what ordinary people can do in response to rapidly advancing AI.
