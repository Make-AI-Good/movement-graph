# Yan Pratama Akhra

Yan Pratama Akhra is the [Program Manager of the AI Safety Professional Training Program](https://aisafety.id/training/) at [AI Safety Indonesia](../local-groups/lg-ai-safety-indonesia.md), a Jakarta-based organization whose flagship program is a three-course professional certification sequence developed in partnership with the Stanford Center for AI Safety. Akhra [serves as the primary contact](https://aisafety.id/contact/) for applicants and enrollees, overseeing a program that prepares Indonesian practitioners to address AI safety risks across government, industry, and academic contexts.
