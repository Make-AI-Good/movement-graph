# Wes Ezzeddine

Wes Ezzeddine is co-founder of [AI Safety UAE](../local-groups/lg-ai-safety-uae.md) and leads its Literacy working group, which coordinates study groups, AI policy guidance, and certification pathways for the UAE's AI safety practitioner community. He [serves as a facilitator with the European Network for AI Safety (ENAIS)](https://www.enais.co/about) — a coordination body linking AI safety researchers and field-builders across Europe — connecting AI Safety UAE's regional community-building work to the broader international practitioner network. His professional background spans more than 15 years of engineering leadership across fintech, insurtech, and mobility, [including a Director of Engineering and acting CTO role at Mamo Pay](https://ae.linkedin.com/in/wes-ezzeddine), the Dubai-based mobile payments platform, and prior engineering management roles at Luko by Allianz Direct and Heetch. Ezzeddine [spoke at the SUMMIT 333 AI Safety conference in Abu Dhabi in November 2025](https://www.summit333.com/speakers), an international gathering that included Max Tegmark and Jaan Tallinn.
