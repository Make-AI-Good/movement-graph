# Alena Dasha Peethala

Alena Dasha Peethala is a chapter co-leader of [Encode Justice Oregon](../local-groups/lg-encode-justice-oregon.md), the Oregon state chapter of [Encode Justice](../organizations/org-encode-justice.md). She was a student at the International School of Beaverton during the chapter's documented formative activities. Public search-snippet content from [her LinkedIn profile](https://www.linkedin.com/in/alenapeethala1/) describes leading a team of students from across Oregon on policy drafting, educational slideshows, and outreach to teachers and community leaders, including presenting sample K-12 AI policies to the Beaverton School District's Chief Information Officer and advocating for standardised computer-science course implementation across the district.
