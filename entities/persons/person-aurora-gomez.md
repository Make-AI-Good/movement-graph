# Aurora Gómez Delgado

Aurora Gómez Delgado is a [psychologist specializing in digital behaviours](https://www.lamarea.com/2025/05/20/aurora-gomez-una-busqueda-con-chatgpt-consume-10-veces-mas-que-una-busqueda-en-el-google-antiguo/) with 25 years of free-software and digital-rights advocacy from Castilla-La Mancha, Spain. She founded [Tu Nube Seca Mi Río](../local-groups/lg-tu-nube-seca-mi-rio.md) in 2024 [in response to Meta's hyperscale data-centre announcement in Talavera de la Reina](https://www.greeneuropeanjournal.eu/dry-land-for-thirsty-data/), and serves as the collective's principal public spokesperson in [Spanish-language and English-language media](https://www.source-material.org/amazon-microsoft-google-trump-data-centres-water-use/).
