# João Lucas Duim

João Lucas Duim is the founder and director of [impactRIO](../local-groups/lg-impact-rio.md), [which he describes as the first AI-safety community group in Brazil](https://forum.effectivealtruism.org/posts/AyYnGXim8X4c5p9FE/1-year-update-on-impactrio-the-first-ai-safety-group-in); he established the group in July 2023 at Fundação Getulio Vargas in Rio de Janeiro and has since directed three structured fellowship cohorts — alignment, advanced alignment, and governance — reaching 26 completions as of mid-2024. He [co-founded LANAIS](https://forum.effectivealtruism.org/posts/DnqFsjiKW4ooC3DK4/lanais-latin-american-network-for-ai-safety-kick-off) (Latin American Network for AI Safety) in 2025, a regional coordination network connecting researchers, students, and organisers across Latin America.
