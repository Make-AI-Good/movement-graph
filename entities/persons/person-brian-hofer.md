# Brian Hofer

Brian Hofer is a privacy advocate and organizer with [Oakland Privacy](../local-groups/lg-oakland-privacy.md). He [advocated for the creation of the Oakland Privacy Advisory Commission](https://oaklandside.org/2021/11/02/lauded-as-a-national-model-some-question-whether-oaklands-privacy-commission-is-working/) and served as the commission's chair from its founding in 2016 until [his resignation in November 2025](https://oaklandside.org/2025/11/12/oakland-privacy-advisory-commission-resignation-brian-hofer-sean-everhart/). He is the executive director of Secure Justice, a nonprofit civil rights and technology organization focused on privacy and police accountability.
