Director at [Fight for the Future](../organizations/org-fight-for-the-future.md), the US digital-rights campaign organization, where they lead campaign strategy and public communications on content regulation, data privacy, commercial surveillance, and algorithmic harms. A queer transgender musician and activist based in Boston — their recording work, including the 2021 album *Spotify is Surveillance*, and a 2019 campaign co-authored with Tom Morello winning facial-recognition-free pledges from major music festivals, connect artistic production directly to the surveillance and AI-accountability work they run at FFTF.
