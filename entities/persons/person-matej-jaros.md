# Matěj Jaroš

Matěj Jaroš is the chairman (Předseda) and co-founder of [PauseAI Czechia](../local-groups/lg-pauseai-czechia.md), the Czech national chapter of [PauseAI](../organizations/org-pauseai.md). [Named by PauseAI Global as Czechia's national leader](https://pauseai.info/about) in the federation's chapter directory, he also serves as the chapter's social-media administrator. A student, [he discovered the international PauseAI movement in spring 2024 and began building the Czech chapter by September of that year](https://denikalarm.cz/2026/06/nastal-cas-zmacknout-pauzu-clenove-pauseai-varuji-soucasny-vyvoj-ai-muze-smerovat-ke-kolapsu/), growing a volunteer team drawing on expertise from information systems, economics, and sociology. He [organised PauseAI Czechia's February 2025 protest at Václavské náměstí (Wenceslas Square)](https://luma.com/6t4fmgw0) in Prague — the chapter's contribution to the federation's international protest wave around the Paris AI Action Summit.
