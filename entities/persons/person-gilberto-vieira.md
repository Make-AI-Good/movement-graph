# Gilberto Vieira

Gilberto Vieira co-founded [data_labe](../local-groups/lg-datalabe.md) in 2016 within the Observatório de Favelas in the Complexo da Maré, Rio de Janeiro. He is the central theorist of data_labe's citizen-generated data methodology, [framing it as "how people can produce data by themselves and be engaged in the production of data without being a formal research institute"](https://rioonwatch.org/?p=56874). His [articulation of the lab's political premise](https://latamjournalismreview.org/articles/brazilian-youth-led-data-journalism-lab-produces-vital-environmental-and-safety-information-in-a-rio-de-janeiro-favela/) — that official data systems "do not take into account the nuances of the periphery, which only we know" — frames the production of favela-generated data as a political act, not a technical one.
