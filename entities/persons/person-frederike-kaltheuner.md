# Frederike Kaltheuner

Frederike Kaltheuner led [Privacy International](../organizations/org-privacy-international.md)'s Data Exploitation Programme, [co-authoring the foundational 2018 joint report](https://privacyinternational.org/press-release/1753/ai-tools-threaten-right-privacy-and-freedom-expression-report) [*Privacy and Freedom of Expression In the Age of Artificial Intelligence*](../publications/pub-article19-pi-privacy-freedom-expression-ai-2018.md) with [ARTICLE 19](../organizations/org-article-19.md). She went on to establish and direct [Human Rights Watch](../organizations/org-human-rights-watch.md)'s Technology and Human Rights Division, serve as Special Adviser to the European Commission Vice-President on digital policy, and found the European AI and Society Fund (a €4.5 million portfolio focused on European AI policy). She holds an MSc in Internet Science from the University of Oxford and currently works independently on EU AI industrial strategy, co-authoring the EU AI Industrial Policy Monitor with the AI Now Institute.
