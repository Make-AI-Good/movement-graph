# Manon Vergerio

Manon Vergerio is a critical urbanist, housing-justice organizer, and Visiting Assistant Professor at Columbia University's Graduate School of Architecture, Planning and Preservation. She [joined the Anti-Eviction Mapping Project](../local-groups/lg-anti-eviction-mapping-project.md) in 2014 and [co-founded its New York City chapter in August 2017](https://notesfrombelow.org/article/anti-eviction-mapping-project), contributing tenant-rights organizing, data analysis, and research on landlord surveillance technologies. She is co-founder and Head of Data & Advocacy at [Unlock NYC](https://www.manonvergerio.com/unlocknyc), a women-led tech nonprofit building digital tools for fair-housing reporting and source-of-income discrimination documentation in New York City. With [Erin McElroy](../persons/person-erin-mcelroy.md) she [co-authored a 2022 peer-reviewed study](https://journals.sagepub.com/doi/abs/10.1177/02637758221088868) on landlord technologies and gentrification in New York City homes.
