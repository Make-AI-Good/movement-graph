# Matilda da Rui

Matilda da Rui is the UK Deputy Director of [PauseAI](../organizations/org-pauseai.md) and co-organizer of [PauseAI London](../local-groups/lg-pauseai-london.md). [Listed on the PauseAI UK homepage](https://pauseai.uk/) as UK Deputy Director, she co-organizes the chapter's protest actions and events alongside UK Director [Joseph Miller](person-joseph-miller.md). She was [identified by TechTimes as deputy director and co-organizer of the June 2025 DeepMind protest](https://www.techtimes.com/articles/311120/20250701/google-deepmind-slammed-protesters-over-broken-ai-safety-promise.htm) and [named by MIT Technology Review](https://www.technologyreview.com/2026/03/02/1133814/i-checked-out-londons-biggest-ever-anti-ai-protest/) as one of the two co-organizers of the February 2026 King's Cross march.
