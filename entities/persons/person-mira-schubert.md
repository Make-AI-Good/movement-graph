# Mira Schubert

Mira Schubert served as Director of Advocacy for [Encode Justice New York](../local-groups/lg-encode-justice-new-york.md), the New York City chapter of [Encode Justice](../organizations/org-encode-justice.md). Her work focused on NYPD surveillance transparency — [partnering with the NYCLU and the Surveillance Technology Oversight Project (S.T.O.P.)](https://aiyouthcscw2023.wordpress.com/) to push for accountability around police surveillance technologies — and on facial recognition regulation in New York schools.
