# Joseph Miller

Joseph Miller is the UK Director of [PauseAI](../organizations/org-pauseai.md) and a primary organizer of [PauseAI London](../local-groups/lg-pauseai-london.md). [Listed as UK Director on the PauseAI UK homepage](https://pauseai.uk/), he was [described by MIT Technology Review at the February 2026 King's Cross march](https://www.technologyreview.com/2026/03/02/1133814/i-checked-out-londons-biggest-ever-anti-ai-protest/) as an Oxford PhD student in mechanistic interpretability. He hosts and co-organizes the chapter's regular London events — social meetups, letter-writing nights, and protest actions — and was a named speaker at the February 2026 march alongside Stuart Russell.
