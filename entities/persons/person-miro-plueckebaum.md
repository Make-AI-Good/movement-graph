# Miro Plueckebaum

Miro Plueckebaum is the [founder of the Singapore AI Safety Hub (SASH)](../local-groups/lg-singapore-ai-safety-hub.md), a Singapore-anchored AI-safety coworking space, events platform, and ecosystem-builder [established in 2025](https://www.scai.gov.sg/2025/participants-of-scai-2025/miro-plueckebaum/). He concurrently serves as Strategy & Research Manager at the Oxford Martin AI Governance Initiative and Programme Specialist at the Centre for the Governance of AI (GovAI). Prior roles include Product and Governance Lead at Conjecture and Product Lead at ByteDance in Beijing and Singapore.
