# Alistair Stewart

Alistair Stewart is a London-based volunteer who [coordinated the first PauseAI protest outside Google DeepMind's London office](https://pauseai.info/2023-may-deepmind-london) in May 2023 — one of PauseAI's earliest public actions anywhere. He organized the action before [PauseAI London](../local-groups/lg-pauseai-london.md) was professionalized with paid staff.
