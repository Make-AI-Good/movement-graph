# Ayoze García González

Ayoze García González is the national director of [PauseAI Spain](../local-groups/lg-pauseai-spain.md), the Spanish national chapter of [PauseAI](../organizations/org-pauseai.md). [Named by PauseAI Global as Spain's national leader](https://pauseai.info/about) in the federation's chapter directory, he leads a chapter notable for its pan-Hispanic scope — extending PauseAI's call for a coordinated pause on frontier AI development to volunteers from both Spain and Hispanic America. García González is a journalist and filmmaker associated with the Pestaña Cíclope collective, and directs the radio programme *Intangible 23* broadcast on Todo Radio.
