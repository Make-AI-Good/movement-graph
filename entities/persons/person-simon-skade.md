# Simon Skade

Simon Skade is co-leader of [PauseAI Germany](../local-groups/lg-pauseai-germany.md), the German national chapter of [PauseAI](../organizations/org-pauseai.md), and an AI safety researcher (KI-Sicherheitsforscher). [Co-registered as Leitungsebene alongside Benjamin Schmidt](https://www.lobbyregister.bundestag.de/suche/R007813) in the German federal Lobbyregister (R007813), he [organised the chapter's Delhi AI Impact Summit petition](https://radiocorax.de/pauseai-ki-sicherheitsabkommen-zum-delhi-gipfel/) in February 2026, which gathered signatures from over 140 German university professors.
