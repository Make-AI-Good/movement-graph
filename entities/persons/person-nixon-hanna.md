# Nixon Hanna

Nixon Hanna is Director of the AI Safety Fundamentals Fellowship (AISF) at [MIT AI Alignment (MAIA)](../local-groups/lg-mit-ai-alignment.md) and a member of the executive board. He is an MIT physics undergraduate and [describes his focus](https://www.nixonhanna.com/) as "ensuring that AI goes well for all of humanity and mitigating catastrophic risk from advanced AI." He is also an Undergraduate Student Researcher at the MIT Kavli Institute for Astrophysics and Space Research.
