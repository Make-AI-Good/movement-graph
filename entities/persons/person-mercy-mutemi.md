# Mercy Mutemi


Nairobi-based litigator; [managing partner](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/ford-foundation-announces-30-new-members-of-ford-global-fellowship-302418390.html) of Kenyan firm [Nzili & Sumbi Advocates](../organizations/org-nzili-sumbi-advocates.md) and founder and executive director of [The Oversight Lab](https://www.fordfoundation.org/news-and-stories/stories/when-technology-meets-justice-nettrice-gaskins-and-mercy-mutemi-on-building-equitable-digital-futures/), an African civil-society response to systemic technology harms that she established in mid-2025. Lead Kenyan counsel in the litigation against Meta and Sama brought by [Daniel Motaung](../persons/person-daniel-motaung.md) and by 185 former Facebook content moderators challenging their mass dismissal — cases on which she works [alongside Foxglove](../organizations/org-foxglove.md) as its [self-described "Kenyan counsel"](https://www.foxglove.org.uk/2024/09/23/facebook-content-moderators-kenya-meta-appeal/), and also lead counsel in the Kenyan suit brought by Ethiopian nationals alleging Facebook's algorithm amplified hate speech during the Tigray war. Named to the [TIME100 Next list in 2023](https://www.codastory.com/authoritarian-tech/mercy-mutemi-meta-lawsuit/) and a 2025 Ford Global Fellow.
