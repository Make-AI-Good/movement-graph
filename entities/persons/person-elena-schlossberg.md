# Elena Schlossberg

Elena Schlossberg is the Executive Director of the [Coalition to Protect Prince William County](../local-groups/lg-coalition-protect-prince-william.md), the Northern Virginia civic coalition opposing hyperscale data-centre development in the Loudoun / Prince William corridor. She [began mobilising Prince William County neighbors in 2014 against a proposed high-voltage transmission line to power an Amazon Web Services data centre](https://progressive.org/magazine/resisting-the-data-center-boom-brant-salazar-20260304/), and after four years of community organising [forced the power company to bury the line along Interstate 66](https://progressive.org/magazine/resisting-the-data-center-boom-brant-salazar-20260304/). She subsequently directed the Coalition's multi-year fight against the Prince William Digital Gateway, which produced [a Virginia Court of Appeals ruling in March 2026 upholding the invalidation of the project's rezoning](https://wjla.com/news/local/prince-william-county-digital-gateway-datacenter-appeal-lawsuit-rezoning-rural-farmland-manassas-battlefield-community-opposition-judges-construction-halted-developer-qts-compass-blackstone-supreme-virginia-economy-environment-landuse) and a [unanimous April 2026 Board of County Supervisors vote to stop defending the project](https://www.washingtontimes.com/news/2026/apr/15/prince-william-county-supervisors-abandon-fight-manassas-data-centers/).
