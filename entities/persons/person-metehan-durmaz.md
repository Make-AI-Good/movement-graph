# Metehan Durmaz

Metehan Durmaz is a Turkey-based Policy Analyst at [SMEX](../organizations/org-smex.md), focusing on IT law, AI and algorithms, and digital-rights issues across the West Asia and North Africa region. He authored the May 2025 [*AI Investments in the Gulf: Opportunities and Surveillance Risks*](../publications/pub-smex-ai-investments-gulf.md) report, and has published SMEX analysis on platform content moderation, privacy in consumer applications, and digital-infrastructure policy in the WANA region.
