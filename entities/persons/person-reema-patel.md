# Reema Patel

UK public-engagement and participation specialist working at the intersection of AI / data governance and democratic deliberation. Patel is a [co-founder of the public-engagement and participation work](https://www.reemapatel.org/) at the [Ada Lovelace Institute](../organizations/org-ada-lovelace-institute.md), where she served as [Associate Director (Engagement)](https://uksa.statisticsauthority.gov.uk/news/involving-people-in-the-use-of-data-for-research-and-official-statistics-heres-how/) and [worked between 2014 and 2022](https://digitalgood.net/people/reema-patel/) on the body of practice — citizen juries, deliberative councils, participatory inquiries — that has become the Institute's distinctive contribution to UK AI governance. While at the Institute she lead-authored the [*Participatory Data Stewardship*](../publications/pub-participatory-data-stewardship.md) framework (2021) and the earlier *Beyond Face Value*, *Rethinking Data* and *The Data Divide* reports, and sat on the UK Statistics Authority's [Centre for Applied Data Ethics independent Advisory Committee](https://uksa.statisticsauthority.gov.uk/news/involving-people-in-the-use-of-data-for-research-and-official-statistics-heres-how/).

After leaving the Institute she founded [Elgon Social Research](https://www.reemapatel.org/), an independent research consultancy on technology, data and AI ethics, public engagement and participation, and equity, where she is Research Director. She is also currently the [Policy and Public Engagement Lead at the ESRC Digital Good Network](https://digitalgood.net/people/reema-patel/) at the University of Sheffield, where she sits on the team behind the 2024-25 [*Participatory and Inclusive Data Stewardship*](https://digitalgood.net/research/participatory-and-inclusive-data-stewardship/) landscape review — co-produced with the Ada Lovelace Institute and the Liverpool City Region Civic Data Cooperative — that explicitly builds on her 2021 Ada Lovelace framework. Alongside the academic work she holds advisory affiliations with the [Bank of England's Central Bank Digital Currency Engagement Forum, the Finance Innovation Lab (Senior Fellow), the Wellcome Trust, and the Scottish Government on COVID-19 public engagement](https://www.reemapatel.org/), and was a delivery partner on Ada Lovelace's UKRI-funded [Public Voices in AI](https://www.adalovelaceinstitute.org/project/public-voice-in-ai/) collaboration (April 2024-March 2025) representing Elgon Social Research.
