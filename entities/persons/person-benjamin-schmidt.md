# Benjamin Schmidt

Benjamin Schmidt is the chair of [PauseAI Germany](../local-groups/lg-pauseai-germany.md), the German national chapter of [PauseAI](../organizations/org-pauseai.md), and a bioinformatician at Martin-Luther-Universität Halle-Wittenberg. [Named by PauseAI Global as Germany's national leader](https://pauseai.info/about) in the federation's chapter directory, he is co-registered alongside Simon Skade as the chapter's Leitungsebene in the [German federal Lobbyregister (R007813)](https://www.lobbyregister.bundestag.de/suche/R007813). He co-organised the chapter's [Appell zum KI-Gipfel 2026](https://pause-ai.de/appell) — the Delhi AI Impact Summit academic appeal that gathered signatures from over 140 German university professors.
