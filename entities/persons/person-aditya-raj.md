# Aditya Raj

Aditya Raj is the Founder of [AI Safety India](../local-groups/lg-ai-safety-india.md), a national Indian community initiative launched in 2025 to build a structured AI-safety education and research pipeline for students and researchers in India. He [documented the founding](https://rajsecrets.medium.com/storyfounding-ai-safety-india-1-18c836842c55) in a multi-part Medium series, citing the observation that many Indians were active in global AI-safety work but no structured India-focused programme existed; he consulted over 40 field builders before launch. He has a background in design and completed four BlueDot AI-safety courses within six months prior to founding the group. In April 2025 he announced Phase 1 of the programme on the EA Forum; the inaugural cohort received over 100 applications and selected 40 participants.
