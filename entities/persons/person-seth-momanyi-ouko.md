# Seth Momanyi Ouko

Seth Momanyi Ouko is the Director and national chapter lead of [PauseAI Kenya](../local-groups/lg-pauseai-kenya.md), the Kenyan chapter of [PauseAI](../organizations/org-pauseai.md). [Named by PauseAI Global as Kenya's national leader](https://pauseai.info/about) in the federation's chapter directory, he coordinates volunteers and community campaigns from the chapter's Nairobi office, overseeing the chapter's Swahili / Kiswahili translation programme for AI safety materials and its university outreach and policymaker-engagement activities.
