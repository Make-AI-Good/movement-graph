# Max Tegmark

Max Tegmark is [Professor of Physics at MIT](https://physics.mit.edu/faculty/max-tegmark/) (joined 2004) and Faculty Advisor to [MIT AI Alignment (MAIA)](../local-groups/lg-mit-ai-alignment.md) and a mentor at the [Cambridge Boston Alignment Initiative (CBAI)](../organizations/org-cambridge-boston-alignment-initiative.md). He co-founded the Future of Life Institute (FLI) in 2014 alongside Jaan Tallinn, Anthony Aguirre, and Viktoriya Krakovna — an organisation focused on reducing global catastrophic risks from advanced technologies including AI. He is the author of two New York Times bestsellers: *Our Mathematical Universe* (2014) and *Life 3.0: Being Human in the Age of Artificial Intelligence* (2017). Time Magazine named him one of the 100 Most Influential People in AI in 2023. His current research focuses on mechanistic interpretability and guaranteed-safe AI via formal verification.
