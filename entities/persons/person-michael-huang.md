# Michael Huang

Michael Huang is co-leader of [PauseAI Australia](../local-groups/lg-pauseai-australia.md), the Australian national chapter of [PauseAI](../organizations/org-pauseai.md). [Named on PauseAI Global's About page](https://pauseai.info/about) alongside [Peter Horniak](person-peter-horniak.md) as one of the chapter's two co-listed national leaders — the only PauseAI national chapter in the federation's directory to carry two co-equal named leaders rather than a single lead.
