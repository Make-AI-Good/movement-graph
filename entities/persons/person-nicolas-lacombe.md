# Nicolas M. Lacombe

Nicolas M. Lacombe (Nik) is the Montréal community coordinator for [PauseAI Canada](../local-groups/lg-pauseai-canada.md), the Canadian national chapter of [PauseAI](../organizations/org-pauseai.md). [Named on PauseAI Canada's Montréal page](https://pauseai.ca/montreal) as the local community contact and named again on the Warning Shot Protocol action page, he also [maintains the chapter's pauseai.ca website](https://github.com/nikthink/pauseai.ca) under the nikthink GitHub handle — with commit history running from October 2025 through April 2026, including the April 2026 landing of the "Warning Shot email campaign feature" for federal MP outreach.
