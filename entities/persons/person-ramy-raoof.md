# Ramy Raoof

Ramy Raoof is an Egyptian digital security researcher, civil society technologist, and activist based in Cairo. Senior Researcher at the [Egyptian Initiative for Personal Rights](../organizations/org-eipr.md) and research collaborator with [Citizen Lab](../organizations/org-citizen-lab.md) (as of 2017); served on the Tor Project Board of Directors (2017–2022); currently Technologist at the Open Technology Fund (OTF). Affiliations, investigations, and public output are recorded on the linked Voice entry.
