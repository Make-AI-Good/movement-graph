# Siri M.

Siri M. is identified as the chapter lead of [Encode Justice North Carolina](../local-groups/lg-encode-justice-north-carolina.md) in [EFF's June 2024 Electronic Frontier Alliance spotlight on the chapter](https://www.eff.org/deeplinks/2024/06/encode-justice-nc-movement-safe-equitable-ai). The source uses only a first name and last initial, consistent with the privacy conventions the chapter applies to its student members. The chapter is open to North Carolina–based high school and college students working on AI policy.
