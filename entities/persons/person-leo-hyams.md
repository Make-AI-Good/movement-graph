# Leo Hyams

Leo Hyams is co-founder and Executive Director of [AI Safety South Africa (AISSA)](../local-groups/lg-ai-safety-south-africa.md), the Cape Town-based AI-safety capacity-building organization he established with Benjamin Sturgeon in 2023. He [authored the September 2025 announcement of the Cooperative AI Research Fellowship](https://forum.effectivealtruism.org/posts/9AmYzjxPAbRgzbgq8/announcing-the-cooperative-ai-research-fellowship) on the EA Forum — the three-month in-person research program AISSA co-organized with the Cooperative AI Foundation, the UCT AI Initiative, and PrincInt. Alongside his AISSA role, Hyams [has served as a lead organizer for the Deep Learning IndabaX South Africa](https://indabax.co.za/archive/indabax2024/), the continent-wide annual machine-learning conference, and co-facilitated the AI safety workshop at its 2024 edition alongside Benjamin Sturgeon.
