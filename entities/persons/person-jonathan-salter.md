# Jonathan Salter

Jonathan Salter founded and [led](https://pauseai.substack.com/p/australian-and-swedish-chapters-launched) [PauseAI Sweden](../local-groups/lg-pauseai-sweden.md), the Swedish national chapter of [PauseAI](../organizations/org-pauseai.md). He is a political science graduate of Stockholm University, Director of Effective Altruism Stockholm since 2019, and co-founder of AI Safety Collab, a non-profit focused on AI safety field-building — roles [described in his speaker biography](https://www.chalmers.se/en/current/calendar/a-history-of-how-the-ai-safety-movement-shot-itself-in-the-foot-and-summoned-the-very-thing-it-feared/) for a Chalmers University seminar in October 2025. He [gave a Swedish-language interview on the SvD Tech Brief podcast](https://www.podplay.com/podcasts/svd-tech-brief-1147425/episodes/han-vill-pausa-utvecklingen-av-ai-for-att-radda-manskligheten-530733450) framing the AI pause case to a Swedish news audience.
