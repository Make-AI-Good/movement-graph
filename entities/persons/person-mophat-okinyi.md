# Mophat Okinyi

Kenyan tech-worker organiser, former Samasource data labeller, and the public-facing anchor of the Nairobi worker-led AI-supply-chain organising layer; [founder and CEO since 2024](https://www.techworkercommunityafrica.com/our-team) of [Techworker Community Africa (TCA)](../organizations/org-techworker-community-africa.md) and [chairperson](https://www.yahoo.com/news/time100-ai-2024-mophat-okinyi-112500518.html) of the [African Content Moderators Union (ACMU)](../organizations/org-african-content-moderators-union.md). His full name on the public record of the [July 2023 parliamentary petition against OpenAI and Sama](../campaigns/camp-mutemi-chatgpt-sama-parliamentary-petition.md) is [Mophat Ochieng Okinyi](https://www.citizen.digital/tech/kenyan-moderators-behind-chatgpt-want-parliament-to-probe-openai-sama-over-exploitation-n323387). He was named to the [TIME100 AI 2024 list](https://www.yahoo.com/news/time100-ai-2024-mophat-okinyi-112500518.html) and is the [2023 Responsible AI Institute "Outstanding Individual" RAISE award winner](https://www.techworkercommunityafrica.com/our-team) — recognised for leadership in responsible AI through research, advocacy, and policy work — and a [2024 Alan Turing Institute AI UK speaker on "Data, Labour, and AI"](https://www.techworkercommunityafrica.com/our-team).

Okinyi joined Sama in Nairobi in [2019 as his first job](https://www.csmonitor.com/World/Africa/2023/0712/African-tech-workers-press-global-social-media-giants-for-better-conditions) and worked there until 2022 in [dual roles as data labeller and quality analyst](https://arxiv.org/html/2512.04269); from 2021 he was assigned for approximately [six months](https://www.csmonitor.com/World/Africa/2023/0712/African-tech-workers-press-global-social-media-giants-for-better-conditions) to the OpenAI-contracted reinforcement-learning-from-human-feedback (RLHF) project that became ChatGPT's safety layer, reading [up to 700 text passages a day](https://www.business-humanrights.org/en/latest-news/kenya-chatgpt-content-moderators-decry-toll-of-ai-model-training-file-government-petition-for-investigation-on-exploitative-conditions/) describing child sexual abuse, bestiality, rape, and graphic violence at an hourly rate of approximately [USD 1.50](https://www.csmonitor.com/World/Africa/2023/0712/African-tech-workers-press-global-social-media-giants-for-better-conditions). After Sama terminated the OpenAI contract in February 2022 he moved briefly to [Majorel on European e-commerce customer-service work](https://www.csmonitor.com/World/Africa/2023/0712/African-tech-workers-press-global-social-media-giants-for-better-conditions); the psychological consequences of the ChatGPT-safety work are the through-line of his subsequent public testimony, recorded in his framings — ["if you put so much dirty content in your mind, it changes you"](https://www.csmonitor.com/World/Africa/2023/0712/African-tech-workers-press-global-social-media-giants-for-better-conditions); ["it has really damaged my mental health"](https://www.business-humanrights.org/en/latest-news/kenya-chatgpt-content-moderators-decry-toll-of-ai-model-training-file-government-petition-for-investigation-on-exploitative-conditions/); ["I lost my family"](https://www.business-humanrights.org/en/latest-news/kenya-chatgpt-content-moderators-decry-toll-of-ai-model-training-file-government-petition-for-investigation-on-exploitative-conditions/) — and in his account of his pregnant wife leaving him during the work because, in her phrasing, he "was a changed man." His settled posture on the disclosure of the work, reported by the Guardian and aggregated through the Business and Human Rights Resource Centre, is the soldier-frame: ["I consider myself a soldier and soldiers take bullets for the good of the people."](https://www.business-humanrights.org/en/latest-news/kenya-chatgpt-content-moderators-decry-toll-of-ai-model-training-file-government-petition-for-investigation-on-exploitative-conditions/)

Okinyi's organising work runs along three connected tracks. He is one of the [six-member founding committee of the ACMU](https://nonprofitquarterly.org/can-african-unions-beat-big-tech/) — voted into existence at the May 2023 Nairobi summit of more than 150 African content moderators for Facebook, TikTok, and ChatGPT — and has served as the union's [chairperson](https://www.yahoo.com/news/time100-ai-2024-mophat-okinyi-112500518.html) and [educational trainer coordinator](https://arxiv.org/html/2512.04269), overseeing its growth to over 400 workers across multiple outsourcing companies. He is one of the [four named petitioners](https://techcrunch.com/2023/07/14/workers-that-made-chatgpt-less-harmful-ask-lawmakers-to-stem-alleged-exploitation-by-big-tech/), alongside Richard Mwaura Mathenge, Alex Mwaura Kairu, and Bill Kelvin Mulinya, on the [July 2023 Mutemi-led parliamentary petition](../campaigns/camp-mutemi-chatgpt-sama-parliamentary-petition.md) asking the Kenyan National Assembly to investigate Sama and the wider Big Tech outsourcing chain — counselled by [Mercy Mutemi](../persons/person-mercy-mutemi.md) of [Nzili & Sumbi Advocates](../organizations/org-nzili-sumbi-advocates.md) and supported by [Foxglove](../organizations/org-foxglove.md). In 2024 he founded [Techworker Community Africa](../organizations/org-techworker-community-africa.md) using his [personal savings](https://www.yahoo.com/news/time100-ai-2024-mophat-okinyi-112500518.html) together with co-founder Richard Mathenge and a four-person founding team, building TCA into a [4,000+ person collective](https://www.techworkercommunityafrica.com/about) that pairs civic education and free psychological support with a hybrid NGO / for-profit / worker-supporting-DAO structure distinct from ACMU's trade-union shape and the [Data Labellers Association](../organizations/org-data-labellers-association.md)'s sectoral-association posture. His distinctive contribution to the make-AI-good movement is the consolidation, across these three tracks, of an organising layer in which the workers who train the world's largest AI systems — the non-AI public that hand-classifies the toxic-content corpora frontier-AI safety depends on — collectively shape the labour, mental-health, and statutory conditions of that work, with his own account of the work as the most extensively reported worker-side testimony in the corpus's coverage of the Kenyan AI supply chain.
