# Teresa Roldán Soria

Teresa Roldán Soria is a co-founder of [Voceras de la Madre Tierra](../local-groups/lg-voceras-de-la-madre-tierra.md), the Querétaro-based women-led environmental-defender collective organising against hyperscale data-centre water extraction in Mexico's primary data-centre corridor. She has been [engaged in environmental defence in Querétaro since 2016](https://www.frontlinedefenders.org/es/case/intimidation-water-and-environmental-defender-teresa-roldan), beginning with the protection of trees and protected natural areas in the former Metropolitan Park zone and widening into water- and territorial-defence work as the state's data-centre industry expanded. Her standing public framing on the water stakes — ["These data centres are in the middle of areas that were used for aquifer recharge, agriculture, or livestock farming, which is a source of income for many families. These centres have taken that income from them"](https://www.context.news/ai/long-read/resistance-blooms-in-mexicos-data-centre-valley) — is the corpus's clearest hydrology-grounded grassroots case against hyperscale AI siting in Latin America. In July 2024 she appeared alongside Voceras co-members at the [joint press conference with Querétaro state deputy Andrea Tovar](https://nativoqueretaro.mx/index.php/2024/07/02/andrea-tovar-y-voceras-de-la-madre-tierra-presentaron-un-posicionamiento-respecto-del-amparo-emitido-por-la-scjn-sobre-la-ley-de-aguas/) following the Supreme Court of Justice's partial overturning of the 2022 State Water Law; the [TRAMAS research network identifies her as having made a formal 2024 intervention before the Court](https://tramas.digital/es/casos/queretaro/) in that litigation. She has faced [death threats and online harassment](https://www.context.news/ai/long-read/resistance-blooms-in-mexicos-data-centre-valley) for her data-centre advocacy and [sustained armed-security-guard intimidation at her home in September–October 2025](https://www.frontlinedefenders.org/es/case/intimidation-water-and-environmental-defender-teresa-roldan) — incidents documented by Front Line Defenders in connection with the Irlanda Apartments development in Querétaro.
