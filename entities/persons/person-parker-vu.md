# Parker Vu

Parker Vu is a team member at [antoan.ai (AI Safety Vietnam)](../local-groups/lg-antoan-ai.md), Vietnam's first AI-safety community organisation, building the local practitioner pipeline through translated resources, a research fellowship, and hackathon events in Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh City. Vu is [listed among the organisation's employees](https://www.linkedin.com/company/antoanai) and [tagged in the Summer Research Fellowship announcement](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/antoanai_aisafetyvietnam-hanoiaisafety-effectivealtruism-activity-7352605957937385472-Mxkw) — a technical programme covering safety evaluations, interpretability, AI control, and adversarial robustness, run in partnership with the Hanoi AI Safety Network.
