# Sahana Srinivasan

Sahana Srinivasan is a chapter co-leader of [Encode Justice Oregon](../local-groups/lg-encode-justice-oregon.md), the Oregon state chapter of [Encode Justice](../organizations/org-encode-justice.md). She was a student at Jesuit High School in Portland during the chapter's documented formative activities. She is named as a co-leader on the chapter's [LinkedIn company page](https://www.linkedin.com/company/encode-justice-oregon-chapter) alongside [Alena Dasha Peethala](person-alena-peethala.md).
