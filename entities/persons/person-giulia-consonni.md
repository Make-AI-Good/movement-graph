# Giulia Consonni

Giulia Consonni is the national leader of [PauseAI Italia](../local-groups/lg-pauseai-italy.md), the Italian national chapter of [PauseAI](../organizations/org-pauseai.md). She is [named on PauseAI Global's about page](https://pauseai.info/about) within the federation's directory of national chapter leads, alongside leads for fourteen other countries including Germany, France, Czechia, Romania, and Serbia.
