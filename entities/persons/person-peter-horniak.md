# Peter Horniak

Peter Horniak is co-leader of [PauseAI Australia](../local-groups/lg-pauseai-australia.md), the Australian national chapter of [PauseAI](../organizations/org-pauseai.md), and the chapter's primary events organiser. [Named on PauseAI Global's About page](https://pauseai.info/about) alongside [Michael Huang](person-michael-huang.md) as one of the chapter's two co-listed national leaders, he is also the [named organiser](https://luma.com/pauseaiaustralia) of PauseAI Australia's public event calendar — including the chapter's Canberra Casual Catchups recurring meetup series, the AI Safety Forum 2026 Camperdown convening, and the PauseAI @NeurIPS Sydney placeholder engagement.
