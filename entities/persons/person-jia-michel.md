# Jia Michel

Jia Michel served as Chapter Director of [Encode Justice New York](../local-groups/lg-encode-justice-new-york.md), the New York City chapter of [Encode Justice](../organizations/org-encode-justice.md). Her advocacy centered on facial recognition regulation in New York schools — [organizing in-person events, meeting with legislators](https://aiyouthcscw2023.wordpress.com/), and collaborating with the NYCLU and the Justice Education Project. She [co-organized the chapter's 2022 Greenpoint event on AI implicit bias](https://northbrooklynnews.com/2022/06/29/event-warns-of-ai-implicit-bias/) and is documented by the CSCW 2023 AI Youth Workshop as chapter director by 2023.
