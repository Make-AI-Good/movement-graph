# Svetozar Janković

Svetozar Janković is the national director of [PauseAI Serbia](../local-groups/lg-pauseai-serbia.md) and a [Docent (Associate Professor of Applied Studies) at the School of Computing (RAF — Računarski Fakultet)](https://raf.edu.rs/en/teaching-staff/dr-svetozar-jankovic/) in Belgrade, appointed to that rank in 2023. He holds a PhD in Technical Sciences from the Faculty of Mechanical Engineering, University of Belgrade (2005) and previously [served as Head of the School of Computers at the Vinča Nuclear Institute](https://raf.edu.rs/en/teaching-staff/dr-svetozar-jankovic/) (2006–2019).
