Founder and executive director of [Hiperderecho](../organizations/org-hiperderecho.md), [leading Peru's principal digital rights organization from its 2013 founding through September 2022](https://hiperderecho.org/2022/09/cambios-en-hiperderecho/). A trained lawyer with an [LL.M. in Law, Science, and Technology from Stanford Law School](https://wilmap.stanford.edu/contributors/miguel-morachimo), Morachimo built Hiperderecho's ¿Quién Defiende Tus Datos? ISP-accountability series in collaboration with [Access Now](../organizations/org-access-now.md) and the [Electronic Frontier Foundation](../organizations/org-electronic-frontier-foundation.md), its civic technology tools (Proyectosdeley.pe, Pidela.info), and its strategic litigation programme; [he cited the multistakeholder environment as central to ISP surveillance and content-blocking accountability in Peru](https://globalnetworkinitiative.org/hiperderecho-joins-gnis-civil-society-constituency/). He transitioned to honorary external advisor in September 2022 on the appointment of his successor Dilmar Villena Fernández Baca.
