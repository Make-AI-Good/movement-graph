# Oliver Hayes

UK campaigner; [Head of Big Tech & Campaigns](https://www.globalactionplan.org.uk/about-us/our-team/oliver-hayes) at [Global Action Plan](../organizations/org-global-action-plan.md) since 2019, following eleven years at Friends of the Earth (where he led post-dieselgate air-pollution work and was part of the successful legal challenge to the Heathrow third-runway approval). Founder of the international End Surveillance Advertising to Kids coalition and a core organiser within [Climate Action Against Disinformation](https://www.globalactionplan.org.uk/about-us/our-team/oliver-hayes) and [#PeopleVsBigTech](https://www.globalactionplan.org.uk/about-us/our-team/oliver-hayes). Lead GAP spokesperson on the [Foxglove / Global Action Plan challenge to UK hyperscale data centres](../campaigns/camp-uk-data-centres.md), including the [August 2025 Woodlands Park judicial-review launch](https://www.globalactionplan.org.uk/insights/news/global-action-plan-and-foxglove-launch-legal-challenge-against-government-decision-to-force-construction-of-a-hyperscale-data-centre) and the [February 2026 UK-wide Dirty Data Centres action days](https://theecologist.org/2026/feb/27/hyperscale-data-centre-protests).
