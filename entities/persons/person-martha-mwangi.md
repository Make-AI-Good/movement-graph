# Martha Mwangi

Martha Mwangi served as chapter leader of [Encode Justice Georgia](../local-groups/lg-encode-justice-georgia.md) at Lambert High School in Suwanee, Forsyth County, Georgia, where she [co-founded the EJ-GA Essay Contest](https://thelambertpost.com/news/encode-justice-scholarship/) alongside Emily Nicholas — a chapter-run scholarship for Georgia high-school students centered on the proposed Georgia Computer Data Privacy Act (SB 394, 2021–2022 session).
