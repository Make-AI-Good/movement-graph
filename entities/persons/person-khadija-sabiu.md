# Khadija Sabiu

Khadija Sabiu is the national chapter lead of [PauseAI Nigeria](../local-groups/lg-pauseai-nigeria.md), the Nigerian chapter of [PauseAI](../organizations/org-pauseai.md). [Named by PauseAI Global as Nigeria's national leader](https://pauseai.info/about) in the federation's chapter directory, she leads the corpus's first West African PauseAI chapter, which advocates for a coordinated global AI pause until robust safety guarantees and democratic accountability are in place — with a particular emphasis on Nigeria building its own AI governance capacity rather than inheriting frameworks designed elsewhere.
