# Jay Stanley

Jay Stanley is Senior Policy Analyst with the [American Civil Liberties Union](../organizations/org-aclu.md)'s Speech, Privacy, and Technology Project, where he researches, writes, and speaks on surveillance technologies and civil liberties — AI video analytics, facial recognition, license plate readers, drone surveillance, and biometric data collection.
