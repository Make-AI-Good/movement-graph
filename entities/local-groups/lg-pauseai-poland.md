# PauseAI Polska

PauseAI Polska is the Polish national chapter of [PauseAI](../organizations/org-pauseai.md), the international AI-safety movement calling for a coordinated, treaty-backed pause on the training of the most powerful general-purpose AI systems. The chapter operates a dedicated Polish-language website at [pauseai.pl](https://pauseai.pl/) and is listed on [PauseAI Global's communities directory](https://pauseai.info/communities) as the Polish national chapter. It is the corpus's second Eastern European PauseAI chapter alongside [PauseAI Czechia](lg-pauseai-czechia.md), closing a named Poland gap in the federation's European footprint.

## Structure and contact

The chapter is listed on the [PauseAI communities page](https://pauseai.info/communities) with contact email `patryk@pauseai.info` — the `@pauseai.info` domain indicating integration into PauseAI International's contact infrastructure, consistent with the pattern observed in most other national chapters. No full-name biographical sourcing for the chapter lead has been located in indexed sources at draft time; a Person entry has not been created. The chapter routes new members through the global `pauseai.info/join` form and into PauseAI International's Discord server, consistent with PauseAI's federated onboarding model.

## Public output at drafting

At the time of drafting, PauseAI Polska's confirmed public-output surface is its [Polish-language chapter website](https://pauseai.pl/), which publishes translated PauseAI International content on AI risk, advocacy pathways (writing to politicians, signing petitions, attending protests), and signatories lists including `superintelligence-statement.org` and `aistatement.com`. The chapter functions as an informational hub and movement on-ramp for Polish audiences.

Poland does not appear in [PauseAI Global's documented protest history](https://pauseai.info/protests) for the major federation protest cycles through early 2025, including the [May 2024 International Day of Action](../events/event-pauseai-international-day-of-action-2024-05-13.md) (thirteen countries) and the [February 2025 Paris AI Action Summit wave](../events/event-pauseai-paris-ai-action-summit-protests-2025-02.md) (eighteen cities). Independent documentation of Poland-specific events or campaigns has not been located in indexed sources at draft time.

## Place in the movement

PauseAI Polska is the corpus's second Eastern European PauseAI entry, joining [PauseAI Czechia](lg-pauseai-czechia.md) — which anchors across Prague and Brno and carries an executed February 2025 protest, a recurring meetup programme, and the Ideathon AI student-engagement programme. Together the two chapters give the corpus its first two-chapter cluster in the post-socialist Central- and Eastern-European AI-safety space. The federation's European footprint in the corpus now spans the UK ([PauseAI London](lg-pauseai-london.md)), France ([Pause IA](lg-pauseai-paris.md)), Germany ([PauseAI Germany](lg-pauseai-germany.md)), the Netherlands ([PauseAI Utrecht](lg-pauseai-utrecht.md)), Czechia, and Poland — with Sweden, Romania, Serbia, and Italy listed on the global communities page but not yet in-corpus at draft time.

PauseAI Polska's documented state at this draft is launch-phase operational: website live, global-directory listing present, contact maintained, community-building infrastructure in place. The pattern is consistent with PauseAI's observed lifecycle for national chapters in their formation phase — the same shape recorded in [PauseAI Kenya](lg-pauseai-kenya.md) and [PauseAI Nigeria](lg-pauseai-nigeria.md) at their respective draft times before events and campaigns accumulated.
