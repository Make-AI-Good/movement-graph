# PauseAI Sweden

PauseAI Sweden is the Swedish national chapter of [PauseAI](../organizations/org-pauseai.md), the international AI-safety movement calling for a coordinated, treaty-backed pause on the training of the most powerful general-purpose AI systems. The chapter operates a dedicated Swedish-language website at [pauseai.se](https://pauseai.se/) and is listed on [PauseAI Global's communities directory](https://pauseai.info/communities) as the Swedish national chapter. PauseAI Sweden was formally announced as a national chapter in PauseAI's [May 2025 newsletter](https://pauseai.substack.com/p/australian-and-swedish-chapters-launched), though activists in Stockholm had already participated in PauseAI's international protest cycles before that formal launch. This entry closes the Sweden advocacy-chapter gap flagged in the [PauseAI Italia](lg-pauseai-italy.md) drafting notes and extends the European PauseAI cluster into Scandinavia.

## Structure and leadership

Jonathan Salter heads PauseAI Sweden, as named in PauseAI's May 2025 newsletter and confirmed by his SvD Näringsliv media profile. Salter is a political science student and long-standing organizer in the Swedish AI-safety and effective-altruism community: he co-founded AI Safety Collab and has directed Effective Altruism Stockholm since 2019. The chapter follows PauseAI's federated national-chapter model, operating under the [org-pauseai](../organizations/org-pauseai.md) parent structure with a country-specific website and localized Swedish-language outreach. No Person entry for Salter exists in the corpus at draft time; one is warranted given his sourcing profile.

## Protest history

Stockholm appeared among the fourteen protest cities in the [13 May 2024 International Day of Action](../events/event-pauseai-international-day-of-action-2024-05-13.md) ahead of the Seoul AI Safety Summit on 21–22 May 2024, placing Sweden in PauseAI's second large-scale international mobilisation and its first multi-continent action. Stockholm was among the European anchor cities alongside London, Berlin, Den Haag, and Paris; no Sweden-specific organizer name or turnout figure has been located in indexed sources.

On 9 February 2025 — within the [Paris AI Action Summit protest wave](../events/event-pauseai-paris-ai-action-summit-protests-2025-02.md) spanning 7–11 February 2025 — PauseAI Sweden staged a protest at Mynttorget in Stockholm's Gamla Stan (old town). Stockholm is listed in [PauseAI Global's February 2025 protest page](https://pauseai.info/2025-february) among the roughly twenty cities coordinated around the Paris AI Action Summit. PauseAI's May 2025 newsletter credited Salter with organizing the Stockholm action within this wave.

## Place in the movement

PauseAI Sweden is the corpus's first Nordic AI-safety advocacy-chapter entry of the PauseAI type, complementing [Et Grønt Holbæk](lg-et-gront-holbaek.md) — which covers Danish data-center opposition — with an AI-pause-moratorium footprint in the Scandinavian cluster. The European PauseAI chapter set in the corpus now spans the UK ([PauseAI London](lg-pauseai-london.md)), France ([Pause IA](lg-pauseai-paris.md)), Germany ([PauseAI Germany](lg-pauseai-germany.md)), the Netherlands ([PauseAI Utrecht](lg-pauseai-utrecht.md)), Czechia ([PauseAI Czechia](lg-pauseai-czechia.md)), Poland ([PauseAI Polska](lg-pauseai-poland.md)), Italy ([PauseAI Italia](lg-pauseai-italy.md)), and now Sweden — an eight-chapter bloc spanning Northern, Western, Central, Eastern, and Southern Europe.

PauseAI Sweden's documented state at draft time is post-launch operational: website live, global-directory listing present, named chapter lead publicly identified and profiled in Swedish media, and protest presence in both the May 2024 and February 2025 international PauseAI waves. The two-wave protest record — one predating the chapter's formal launch announcement and one tied to it — distinguishes PauseAI Sweden from the formation-phase chapters like [PauseAI Polska](lg-pauseai-poland.md) and marks it at a comparable operational stage to [PauseAI Italia](lg-pauseai-italy.md) at its draft time.
