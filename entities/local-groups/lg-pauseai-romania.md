# PauseAI Romania

PauseAI Romania is the Romanian national chapter of [PauseAI](../organizations/org-pauseai.md), the international AI-safety movement calling for a coordinated, treaty-backed pause on the training of the most powerful general-purpose AI systems. The chapter is listed on [PauseAI Global's communities directory](https://pauseai.info/communities) as "România" with a Facebook page as its primary public contact point, and is the corpus's first Southeastern European local group of any kind — extending the Eastern European cluster of PauseAI national chapters that now also includes [PauseAI Czechia](lg-pauseai-czechia.md) and [PauseAI Polska](lg-pauseai-poland.md).

## Leadership and structure

PauseAI Romania is named on [PauseAI Global's About page](https://pauseai.info/about) under a single national-leader listing for **[Raluca Spataru](../persons/person-raluca-spataru.md)**, alongside the federation's other national-chapter leads — [Holly Elmore](../persons/person-holly-elmore.md) (US), [Joseph Miller](../persons/person-joseph-miller.md) (UK), Clémence Peyrot (France), [Benjamin Schmidt](../persons/person-benjamin-schmidt.md) (Germany), [Ayoze García González](../persons/person-ayoze-garcia-gonzalez.md) (Spain), [Giulia Consonni](../persons/person-giulia-consonni.md) (Italy), Carl (Sweden), [Matěj Jaroš](../persons/person-matej-jaros.md) ([PauseAI Czechia](lg-pauseai-czechia.md)), [Khadija Sabiu](../persons/person-khadija-sabiu.md) ([PauseAI Nigeria](lg-pauseai-nigeria.md)), [Seth Momanyi Ouko](../persons/person-seth-momanyi-ouko.md) ([PauseAI Kenya](lg-pauseai-kenya.md)), [Svetozar Jankovic](../persons/person-svetozar-jankovic.md) (Serbia), [Jeremy Eliosoff](../persons/person-jeremy-eliosoff.md) (Canada), and [Michael Huang](../persons/person-michael-huang.md) and [Peter Horniak](../persons/person-peter-horniak.md) (Australia). No further biographical sourcing for Spataru has been located in independently indexed sources beyond the PauseAI About page listing.

The chapter's primary public digital presence is its [Facebook page](https://www.facebook.com/people/Pause-AI-Romania/61581783381263/), which is the contact format listed on the [PauseAI communities directory](https://pauseai.info/communities). This places PauseAI Romania in the social-media-anchored listing tier — alongside PauseAI Kenya (X / Twitter) and PauseAI Nigeria (WhatsApp) — rather than the dedicated-national-website tier used by the Czechia, Germany, France, Spain, Sweden, Australia, Canada, UK, and US chapters. Full page content is authentication-gated and not accessible without a Facebook login at draft time.

## Public output at drafting

## Place in the movement

PauseAI Romania is the corpus's first Southeastern European PauseAI entry and the corpus's first local group of any kind anchored in the Southeastern European region — closing the named gap the Synthesizer queued. The Eastern European cluster in the corpus now spans Czechia ([PauseAI Czechia](lg-pauseai-czechia.md), anchored across Prague and Brno with an executed February 2025 protest, a recurring meetup programme, and the Ideathon AI student-engagement programme) and Poland ([PauseAI Polska](lg-pauseai-poland.md), launch-phase with a dedicated Polish-language website) — Romania adds the first Southeastern European node to that cluster. Serbia is also listed on the [PauseAI communities directory](https://pauseai.info/communities) with a dedicated website and Svetozar Jankovic as chapter lead, but is not yet in-corpus at draft time.

Within the PauseAI federation's observed lifecycle, PauseAI Romania's documented state is formation-phase: listed on the global directory, a named chapter lead, a social-media presence, and no independently-documented protest or event output yet accumulated. This mirrors the formation-phase profile of [PauseAI Kenya](lg-pauseai-kenya.md) and [PauseAI Nigeria](lg-pauseai-nigeria.md) at their respective draft times, before those chapters' public-event and campaign output accumulated.
