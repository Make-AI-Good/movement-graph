# PauseAI Italia

PauseAI Italia is Italy's national chapter of [PauseAI](../organizations/org-pauseai.md), the international AI-safety movement calling for a coordinated, treaty-backed pause on the training of the most powerful general-purpose AI systems. The chapter operates a dedicated Italian-language website at [pauseai.it](https://www.pauseai.it/) and is listed on [PauseAI Global's communities directory](https://pauseai.info/communities) as the Italian national chapter. This entry is the corpus's first Italian local-group entry and closes a named Southern European gap in the federation's PauseAI chapter coverage, extending the European PauseAI cluster into the Mediterranean for the first time.

## Structure and contact

The chapter is listed on the [PauseAI communities page](https://pauseai.info/communities) with a WhatsApp group as the primary contact channel — consistent with the pattern used by PauseAI's volunteer-led national chapters in their formation phase. No named chapter lead has been located in indexed public sources at draft time; a Person entry has not been created. The chapter's social-media footprint spans Facebook (@Pause.IA), X/Twitter, Instagram, TikTok, and Threads (all @pause_ia), LinkedIn (@pause-ia), YouTube (@Pause_IA), and Substack — a multi-platform presence matching the breadth of the larger established European PauseAI national chapters including [Pause IA](lg-pauseai-paris.md) and [PauseAI Germany](lg-pauseai-germany.md).

## Public output at drafting

At the time of drafting, PauseAI Italia's confirmed public-output surface is its [Italian-language chapter website](https://www.pauseai.it/) and documented participation in two PauseAI Global international protest waves — with protest presence in two distinct Italian cities, Rome and Milan, in successive cycles.

- **13 May 2024 — International Day of Action, Seoul AI Summit.** Rome appeared among the fourteen protest cities in the [May 2024 International Day of Action](../events/event-pauseai-international-day-of-action-2024-05-13.md), [staged nine days ahead of the Seoul AI Safety Summit](https://pauseai.info/2024-may). The thirteen-country footprint made this the campaign's first multi-continent mobilisation; Rome was among the European anchor cities alongside London, Berlin, Stockholm, Den Haag, and Paris.

- **November 20–22, 2024 — AI Safety Conference protest wave.** Milan was [listed among the protest locations](https://pauseai.info/2024-november) for PauseAI's international action coordinated around the AI Safety Conference in San Francisco. Coordination for the Milan action ran through the chapter's WhatsApp community link; no organiser name or turnout figure has been independently documented at draft time.

The chapter's website also lists a Riprendere il Controllo (Retaking Control) solutions forum among its campaigns — a named initiative parallel in framing to [Pause IA](lg-pauseai-paris.md)'s Reprendre le Contrôle forum convened in Paris alongside the February 2025 AI Action Summit. [PauseAI Global's protest history](https://pauseai.info/protests) does not document additional Italy-specific protest cycles beyond the two listed above at draft time.

## Place in the movement

PauseAI Italia is the corpus's first Italian entry and closes the named Italy gap flagged in [PauseAI Polska](lg-pauseai-poland.md)'s drafting notes. The European PauseAI cluster in the corpus now spans the UK ([PauseAI London](lg-pauseai-london.md)), France ([Pause IA](lg-pauseai-paris.md)), Germany ([PauseAI Germany](lg-pauseai-germany.md)), the Netherlands ([PauseAI Utrecht](lg-pauseai-utrecht.md)), Czechia ([PauseAI Czechia](lg-pauseai-czechia.md)), Poland ([PauseAI Polska](lg-pauseai-poland.md)), and Italy — a seven-chapter European bloc covering the UK, Continental Western, Central, Eastern, and now Southern Europe, with Sweden, Romania, and Serbia still listed on the global communities page but not yet in-corpus at draft time.

PauseAI Italia's documented state at this draft is launch-phase operational: website live, global-directory listing present, multi-platform social-media infrastructure in place, and protest participation in both the May 2024 and November 2024 international waves. The presence of two distinct protest cities — Rome in May 2024 and Milan in November 2024 — is notable within the corpus's PauseAI chapter set: it shows the Italian chapter engaging across both the national capital and the commercial centre in successive protest cycles, a two-city footprint no other single PauseAI chapter in the corpus matches at a comparable stage of documentation. The pattern of WhatsApp-coordinated community and the absence of a named chapter lead in public records matches the formation-phase profile recorded for [PauseAI Kenya](lg-pauseai-kenya.md) and [PauseAI Nigeria](lg-pauseai-nigeria.md) at their respective draft times before events and campaigns accumulated.
