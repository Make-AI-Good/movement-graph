# PauseAI Spain

PauseAI Spain (Spanish: *PauseAI España*, pauseai.es) is the Spanish national chapter of [PauseAI](../organizations/org-pauseai.md), the international movement calling for a coordinated, treaty-backed pause on the training of the most powerful general-purpose AI systems. The chapter is notable among PauseAI national chapters for its [explicitly pan-Hispanic scope](https://pauseai.es): rather than serving Spain alone, it describes itself as grouping "Spanish-speaking volunteers from Spain and Hispanic America" (*voluntarios hispanohablantes de PauseAI, desde España e Hispanoamérica*) — extending the chapter's remit across the twenty-plus Spanish-speaking countries of Latin America. PauseAI Spain is among the minority of PauseAI national chapters to maintain a dedicated national website with original Spanish-language content rather than a social-media-only contact presence.

## Leadership and structure

The chapter's national director is **Ayoze García González**, [named on PauseAI Global's About page](https://pauseai.info/about) alongside the federation's other 14 national-chapter leads. No founding date is stated on the [website](https://pauseai.es) or the [Substack](https://pauseaispanish.substack.com). The chapter [describes its membership](https://pauseai.es/nosotros) as including "workers and experts in AI, computing, neuroscience, communication, and teaching," framing the volunteer base as multidisciplinary. The website notes content was developed as "local adaptation and translation" of the international PauseAI site.

## Platform — pause on advanced AI

[PauseAI Spain's central demand](https://pauseai.es) is a pause on the development of advanced AI systems beyond GPT-4 capability — specifically AI that "possesses autonomy and agency." The chapter frames this alongside pandemic prevention and nuclear disarmament as equivalent global-priority existential-risk reduction work: "do not allow AI companies to play with our future." The platform follows PauseAI Global's [treaty-backed pause proposal](https://pauseai.info/proposal) without articulating Spain-specific demands analogous to, for example, [PauseAI Serbia](lg-pauseai-serbia.md)'s four Serbia-framed conditions or [PauseAI Germany](lg-pauseai-germany.md)'s "Rote Linien" formulation.

## Channels and media presence

The chapter operates a multi-channel Spanish-language media presence: [X / Twitter (@pauseai_es)](https://pauseai.es), YouTube (@pauseai-es), a [Substack newsletter (*PauseAI en español*)](https://pauseaispanish.substack.com) publishing commentary on advanced AI risk, Discord, WhatsApp, and Spotify. Contact is via contacto@pauseai.es. The Substack self-description — "from Spain and Hispanic America, we join the international PauseAI initiative" — anchors the chapter's cross-continental audience framing in its publication's own voice. No Spanish-language protest events appeared on [PauseAI Global's documented protest history](https://pauseai.info/protests) at draft time; the chapter does not appear in the May 2024 thirteen-country international wave or the February 2025 Paris AI Action Summit wave.

## Place in the movement

PauseAI Spain is the corpus's first Southern European PauseAI chapter entry beyond France and Italy, and the first PauseAI chapter entry with an explicitly pan-Hispanic rather than country-specific scope. Its distinctive contribution to the corpus's PauseAI cluster is geographical reach: by formally addressing both Spain and Hispanic America, the chapter is structurally positioned to connect the global PauseAI network to a twenty-plus-country Spanish-speaking audience that other national chapters do not systematically serve. This cross-continental remit is the chapter's clearest point of differentiation within the federation — most PauseAI national chapters are bounded by the national context implied by their name. Within the corpus's Southern European cluster, PauseAI Spain sits alongside [PauseAI Italy](lg-pauseai-italy.md); the closest analog for pan-national Spanish-language AI-safety organizing in the corpus is [Pause IA (PauseAI France)](lg-pauseai-paris.md)'s French-language reach into Francophone Europe and Africa, though PauseAI Spain's Latin American framing covers a larger and more dispersed linguistic community.
